# Retail Sales & Profit Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to understand revenue performance, profitability and regional sales trends.

## Tools Used
Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Excel

## Dashboard Pages
- Executive Summary
- Sales & Profit by Region
- Product Category Performance
- Monthly Sales Trends

## Key Insights

- While Australia generated the highest profit and sales, the United States had the highest orders overall.
- The profit and sales increased greatly in 2016.
- The water bottle was the top-selling product and also the most returned product
- Sales increase during the end-of-year holiday season.
- A small group of customers contributes a large share of revenue.
